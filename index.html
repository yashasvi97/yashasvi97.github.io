Hi there, I'm Yashasvi Baweja!



I'm a third year Computer Science undergraduate student at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, IIIT-Delhi. My research and academic interests primarily lie in the field of computer vision, pattern recognition, biometrics and artificial intelligence.

I am currently working with Image Analysis and Biometrics (IAB) Lab, under the supervision of Dr. Richa Singh and Dr. Mayank Vatsa. My research revolves around recognizing faces from different domains using metric learning.

I have previously worked as a summer research intern at Indian Institute of Technology, Mandi with Dr. Varun Dutt. My work there involved building a better landslide prediction model using class imbalance techniques (like random oversampling and SMOTE) for Mandi, Himachal Pradesh area.

Apart from my technical interests, I am a big sports enthusiast! I am an avid tennis fan and really enjoy playing the sport whenever I get time.
